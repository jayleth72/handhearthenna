About Us

At Hand and Heart Body Art, we believe in celebrating art and nature together. Our henna tattoos and eco-friendly face paintings are perfect for any celebration, providing a touch of personal expression and joy. Our materials are sourced from natural ingredients, ensuring safety and sustainability.

Founded by Jane Doe, a passionate artist with a deep love for botanical dyes and eco-friendly practices, Hand and Heart began as a small booth at local festivals and has grown into a sought-after service for weddings, parties, and corporate events. Jane's commitment to quality and eco-consciousness drives every aspect of our services.
